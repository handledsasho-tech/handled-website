FAQ

Is this a real person or an AI bot? +
A real person. Every message you send is read and answered by a human assistant. We use technology behind the scenes to help our team work faster, but you're always dealing with a real person who knows your preferences.
What counts as one request? +
One request is one specific action — book a restaurant, order flowers, research hotels, arrange a taxi. Any follow-ups needed to complete that task are included; only a new or changed instruction from you counts as a separate request. Each separate errand (for example, a different gift recipient) is its own request. Anything bigger is quoted upfront before we start.
What are your hours? +
Daily from 7am to 11pm Dubai time. Requests sent outside these hours are picked up first thing the next morning. Full 24/7 coverage is coming later as a free upgrade — no price increase.
Do I pay Handled. for the vendors too? +
No. You always pay vendors directly — the restaurant, the florist, the hotel. Handled. charges only your monthly subscription. We never touch your money for vendor payments.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no notice period, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime and your subscription simply doesn't renew the following month.
How does the free trial work? +
Your first 14 days are free. A valid payment method is required to start. Unless you cancel before day 14, your subscription begins automatically. Cancel anytime before then — no questions asked.
