Why we exist

Dubai is one of the world's most dynamic cities — but living here takes work. Restaurants to book, deliveries to coordinate, appointments to schedule, services to find. It never stops.

We spent years watching people — smart, successful, busy people — lose hours every week to this. Not because they wanted to. Because there was no better option.

Handled. is that better option. A real personal assistant, available on WhatsApp, who handles the details so you can focus on what actually matters.

We're not an app. We're not a chatbot. We're a team of real people who know Dubai, know your preferences, and take responsibility for every task we handle.

How we think

01
People, not technology
AI can search. AI can draft. But AI can't call a restaurant on your behalf, negotiate with a supplier, or make a judgment call when something doesn't go as planned. People can. Every task at Handled. is handled by a human who takes responsibility.
02
Less friction, more done
We obsess over removing unnecessary steps. One message. No forms, no portals, no back-and-forth. You message us, we act, you get results. The simpler the experience, the more time you save.
03
Get better over time
Every task makes us more useful to you. We learn your preferences, your budget, your habits. After a month, we anticipate needs you haven't expressed. After six months, we're an extension of how you live.
What makes Handled. different

Our team has deep roots in Dubai — the restaurants that are actually worth booking, the vendors who deliver what they promise, the shortcuts that save real time. That local knowledge is something no algorithm can replicate.
