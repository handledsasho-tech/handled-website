How It Works

From the moment you message us to the moment the task is done — here's exactly what happens.

Every task, step by step
01

You send a message on WhatsApp

No forms, no app to download, no account to create. Just message us the way you'd message a friend. Any time — day or night. In English or Arabic.

"Book a table for 4 at Zuma tonight, 8pm, it's a business dinner."
02

Your assistant reads your request and acts

A real person reads your message. They already know your preferences from your profile — your usual budget, your favourite areas, your dietary restrictions. They don't ask unnecessary questions. They act.

Your assistant checks availability, calls the restaurant if needed, and handles any special requests.
03

You receive options or a confirmation

For simple tasks: direct confirmation. For tasks with choices (hotels, gifts, travel): 3 curated options presented clearly. You choose, we execute.

"Table confirmed at Zuma DIFC, 8pm, 4 guests. They have your name. Let me know if anything changes."
04

You pay the vendor directly

Handled. never handles your money for vendor payments. You receive a payment link directly from the vendor — the restaurant, florist, hotel. You pay them. We coordinate everything else.

For free reservations: nothing to pay. For orders with payment: vendor sends link directly to you.
05

Your assistant follows up

We don't just book and disappear. We send reminders, confirm deliveries, check on time-sensitive tasks. And we update your profile so next time, we know even more about what you need.

Reminder sent 2 hours before your reservation. Profile updated: "prefers quiet corner, business dinners."
